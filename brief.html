About the Talk

AI often feels like a black box owned by the Global North. But in Latin America, where resilience and "hacking" limits is cultural, AI is becoming a tool for radical democratization.

This talk presents a dual perspective: building massive AI systems for brands like Google and Meta at Superside, while simultaneously teaching local artists to "humanize code" through DataRaíz in Buenos Aires.

It explores how shifting from "technical execution" to "dynamic orchestration" allows creatives in emerging markets to build their own tools, not just use them. A roadmap for bridging top-tier global operations and grassroots artistic expression.

Structure (30 min)

The Context — 5 min

Contrasting the Global Agency world (Superside) with the Latin American Lab (DataRaíz). Setting the dual perspective.

The "Hack" — 15 min

A live breakdown of a creative workflow showing how to build a custom AI tool in real-time to solve a complex artistic problem.

The Future — 10 min

A visual showcase of results from LATAM creators and artists, proving how these methods democratize access to high-end production.

Key Takeaways

System Design as Skill

Shift focus from manual production to designing "dynamic orchestration" workflows that scale creativity without losing the human touch.

Democratize via Tool-Building

Use AI agents and code assistants to create bespoke creative tools, breaking dependence on standard software presets.

Bridge the Gap

Merge "Creative Operations" efficiency with "Artistic Practice." Ensure technology serves the concept—from LATAM grassroots to global scale.

Topics Covered

The LATAM Experience

How resource-constrained ecosystems breed innovation. In Latin America, AI becomes a democratizing force—proving that innovation thrives where ingenuity is abundant.

AI Agents & Creative Code

Demystifying programming through AI Agents and MCP. Leveraging LLMs as coding partners for visual platforms like TouchDesigner and Python.

Immersive Design & Spatial Computing

From large-scale installations to WebXR and AR. How custom AI-driven tools translate digital assets into spatial experiences.

Humanizing Code

Challenging the narrative that AI is just for efficiency. Exploring how it can be humanized for poetic expression—merging intuition with machine scale.
