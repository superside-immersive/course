TALK

The New Creative Reality

Context

Professional Ubiquity

AI assistance has become an operational standard across creative industries. The contemporary market demands professionals who can adapt to this ubiquity, requiring a shift from adaptation to mastery.

Concept

Redefining "Being Creative"

The industry is shifting from technical execution to dynamic orchestration. Intelligence expands the creative horizon, granting individual creatives the architectural power to build complex systems previously out of reach.

Reflection

Critical Thinking

Technical adaptation is insufficient. We need to foster a critical capacity, understanding the implications of this paradigm shift rather than merely following trends.

A Dual Perspective

The Global Agency

As Lead Creative Technologist at Superside, building massive AI systems for brands like Google and Meta. Operating at the intersection of creative operations and enterprise-scale technology.

The LATAM Lab

Through DataRaíz in Buenos Aires, teaching local artists to "humanize code." Where resilience and hacking limits is cultural, AI becomes a tool for radical democratization.

Bridging the Gap

This talk bridges two often separated worlds: high-efficiency corporate structures and the experimental, resource-constrained artistic ecosystem of Latin America. Innovation thrives where ingenuity is abundant.

What We'll Explore

From Users to Architects

Moving beyond the popular AI discourse. Shifting from "technical execution" to "dynamic orchestration"—where creatives stop being software users and start building their own systems.

The LATAM Experience

In Latin America, where hacking limits is cultural, AI becomes a democratizing force. Sharing how resource-constrained ecosystems breed innovation—and what the global industry can learn from it.

Humanizing Code

Challenging the narrative that AI is just for efficiency. Exploring how it can be humanized for poetic expression—merging human intuition with machine scale, from grassroots labs to global studios.

Immersive Frontiers & AI

The Landscape

  • The Intersection: Mapping where Immersive Design and Artificial Intelligence converge today.
  • Exploration: From large-scale installations (TeamLab, Moment Factory) to WebXR and AR—how AI tools are currently deployed to enhance these experiences.
  • Insight: Identifying where "Creative Coding" and AI assistance can unlock new possibilities in immersive concepts.

AI Agents & Code

Coding with Agents

  • The Idea: Demystifying programming through the use of AI Agents and MCP (Model Context Protocol).
  • Approach: Leveraging LLMs as sophisticated coding partners to write scripts for visual platforms (TouchDesigner / Python).
  • Takeaway: How we can direct AI Agents to build the logic we need for expression, becoming technical directors of our own code.

Custom Workflows

Professional Pipelines

  • The Challenge: Designing tailored workflows for Immersive Design contexts.
  • Methodology: Moving away from presets. Custom pipelines inspired by studio workflows that meet commercial standards.
  • Real-World Impact: How these custom tools scale for interactive installations or large-scale immersive projects at industry-leading studios.

Immersive Concepts

Designing for Space

  • The Question: How do we translate digital assets into spatial experiences?
  • Exploration: A conceptual deep-dive into AR, VR, and physical installations. How does digital art inhabit a room?
  • Vision: Using custom-built tools to alter the perception of a space, mixing the virtual with the physical.

The Bigger Picture

Process Over Product

  • The Shift: AI is not just a generator—it's a structural component of the creative process.
  • What Matters: The "System" you build is as important as the final visual artifact. The process is the work.
  • Reflection: How do we evaluate creativity when the tools themselves are intelligent? A conversation worth having.

Democratizing Creative AI

The Thesis

From Users to System Architects

We must transition from being software users to system architects. Democratization happens when creatives gain the agency to build their own tools, merging human intuition with machine scale.

LATAM Perspective

Where Ingenuity is Abundant

AI often feels like a black box owned by the Global North. But in Latin America, where "hacking" limits is cultural, AI becomes a tool for radical democratization. From Buenos Aires to Tokyo, a "glocal" view on technology.

The Challenge

Humanizing Code

This challenges the narrative that AI is just for efficiency. It can be humanized for poetic expression. Technology must serve the concept rather than dictate it—ensuring the human touch is never lost.

Technological Framework

Stack Agnostic

The course teaches the methodology, not just the tool. Whether using Blender, Unity, or TouchDesigner, students learn to adapt AI assistance to their preferred software environment.

Code Assisted

Leveraging AI assistants to write shaders, scripts, and logic. This democratizes access to complex effects, allowing students to focus on "what" to create, not "how" to type it.

Real-Time Immersion

Introduction to Immersive Design concepts, utilizing WebXR and Real-Time rendering engines to deploy creative work into spatial contexts.
